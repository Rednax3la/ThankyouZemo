My Experience in Simple Terms

🏢 What is Zemo Card Kenya?

Zemo Card Kenya is a technology company in Nairobi that helps businesses manage their money better. They create software tools that make it easier for companies to track their expenses and payments.

🎯 What Did I Do There?

I worked as a software developer intern for a few months. My main job was to help build computer applications that businesses can use to organize their work and track their projects. Think of it like creating a digital workspace where teams can see their tasks, deadlines, and progress all in one place.

🥅 My Goal

My main objective was to apply my Data Science skills to design insightful dashboards. This goal was exceptionally valuable and enriching, allowing me to bridge data science with practical business applications.

💻 What Skills Did I Learn?

Frontend Development: I learned to create the parts of websites that users see and interact with - like buttons, menus, and colorful dashboards. I used modern tools to make these interfaces look professional and easy to use.

Backend Development: I worked on the "behind-the-scenes" part of applications - the logic that makes everything work properly, stores data safely, and connects different parts of the system together.

Database Management: I learned how to organize and store large amounts of business data in a way that makes it easy to find and use later.

🌟 What Did I Build?

I helped create several important features:

  • A dashboard that shows project progress at a glance
  • A system for organizing and tracking tasks
  • Visual charts that help teams understand their work timelines
  • Tools that work on both computers and mobile phones

🚀 Why Was This Important?

This experience taught me how technology can solve real business problems. I went from learning about programming in school to actually building tools that help real companies work more efficiently. It was like going from reading about cooking to actually preparing meals in a professional kitchen!

🙏 What I'm Most Grateful For

The team at Zemo Card Kenya treated me like a valued team member, not just an intern. They shared their knowledge, gave me real responsibilities, and helped me grow both professionally and personally. This experience has prepared me to tackle bigger challenges in the tech industry.
